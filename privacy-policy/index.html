Privacy Policy

Data collected

I do not collect any data. Everything is under the control of the user's browser.

Data stored on the user's browser

A small string outlining where to redirect the user for following on their own instance to is stored on the browser the purpose of which is to simplify the process of following a user on recurring visits. This data is never sent to any server at any point.

While technically possible, a future update could prompt the users browser to send this data to a server, but this is not planned, and I vow to make any such future change opt-in, should it happen at all.

What about analytics/metrics?

Due to the simple nature of this service, we do not collect any analytics or metrics. We do not even have a server to collect any data on.
